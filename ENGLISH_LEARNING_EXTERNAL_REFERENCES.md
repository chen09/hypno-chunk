## Practical Reuse in HypnoChunk

- Use news videos as source input.
- Keep learning units short (word/phrase/sentence first).
- Avoid long paragraph chunking; force sentence or clause-level bilingual alternation.
- Add spaced-review layer once forgetting-curve scheduling is implemented.
